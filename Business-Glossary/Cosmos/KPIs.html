Business KPIs

The following topics provide a quick reference to how each KPI is used, where each fits into the Cosmos Solution what question it helps answer, the calculation used to figure it, and how it is populated.

Transaction based KPIs: 

The Transaction based KPIs give insight into the customer data at the granularity level of transactions.

 

Customer Demography KPI: 

The Customer Demography KPI gives insights into the customer’s basic details like Age, Gender, Income, Occupation, Marital Status, Ethnicity, Address.

 

Customer Preferences KPI:

 The Customer Preferences KPI gives deep insights into the customer’s inclination in their purchase pattern right from their choice of device, channel, payment-method to the products.

 

Digital KPI: 

Digital KPI gives insights into cyber-behavior of the customers including their browsing and buying pattern.

 

Customer Lifestyle KPI: 

Customer Lifestyle KPI gives insights into customer’s routine, habits and their way of life that would determine their purchase-pattern.

 

Marketing KPI: 

The Marketing KPI gives insights into promotions and their effectiveness in making the customers visit the app/web and purchase more often.
