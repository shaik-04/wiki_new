Cosmos: Customer 360

The customer 360 platform is developed with a vision to have a single view around customer’s behavior which can be broadly categorized into transactional, digital (browse based), marketing, demographics, lifecycle, and predictive features and can be used within the organization to better understand their customers so that a superior customers experience can be delivered.

Genome can be leveraged by multiple teams within an organization like marketing, engagement, sales, commerce, service etc. where it can help these teams to derive value, achieve sustainable competitive advantage, and maximize new customer acquisition opportunities whether in-store or online.

Genome can be of great value for the below use cases – 

  • Provide superior customer experiences that increase the likelihood of revisits.
  • Use data insights to segment at scale and generate personalize discount coupons and direct marketing offers.
  • Increase profitability by reducing the spends on directionless marketing.
  • Increase transaction and cart values by recommending companion products of preferred choice at checkout. 
  • Predict customer’s patterns and give insights on customer’s worth.
  • Improve accessibility of the data to feed the analytics and ML initiatives, broadening the downstream activities.
