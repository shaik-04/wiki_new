Transaction based KPIs

The Transaction based KPIs give insight into the customer data at the granularity level of transactions.

 

1. Recency KPI: 

The number of days since the last purchase for a customer. Recency KPI is calculated by

(Current date – Order date of the most recent order)

2. Frequency KPI: 

The number of transactions of a customer in the past one year. Frequency KPI is calculated by

(The total number of orders corresponding to each Customer ID in the last one year)

3. Customer Spends KPI: 

The overall annual spending of a customer. Customer Spends KPI is calculated by

(Sum of all the Sales Prices of a customer)

4. Average Order gap KPI: 

The average number of days between purchases for a customer. The average order gap KPI is calculated by 

(Last Transaction date - First Transaction date)/ Total number of transactions

5. New Existing Flag KPI: 

This Flag explains if the customer is new or an existing one. New customers are those who have made their first transaction in the last 90 days. Existing customers are those who have made more than one transaction.

6. Active Inactive Status Flag KPI: 

This flag explains the active-inactive status of the customer. Customers are deemed inactive if their last transaction is not made in the last 90 days.

7. Customer Tenure KPI: 

Customer Tenure KPI shows the number of days since the first transaction for a customer. Customer Tenure KPI is calculated by

(Current date – Customer’s First Purchase date)

8. Savings Discounts KPI: 

Total Discounts, Promotions & Rewards applied across purchases & their percentages compared to total sales. This KPI is calculated by

(Sum of discounts / Sum of sales) * 100

9. Bulk Item KPI: 

Customer buying multiple units comprising of large basket sizes and value. Average Basket Size and Average basket value is 95 Percentile meanwhile this KPI shows the top 5 percentile.

10. Premium Buyer KPI: 

A customer is a premium buyer, if they buy at least one Premium Product in any of the Categories.

11. Loyalty Member KPI: 

Customers who own a rewards credit card.

12. Loyalty Type KPI: 

The existing membership tier held by the customer. When Customer Tier is 1,2,3, then Loyalty type is Regular, Prive, Elite respectively.

13. Lifetime Average Order Value KPI: 

The average order value of all the transactions of a customer in their lifetime. This KPI is calculated by

(Sum of sales of all transactions / Total number of transactions)

14. Lifetime Total Items KPI: 

Total number of items purchased by a customer in their lifetime.

15. Tap Rate KPI: 

Total no. of distinct item types / SKUs purchased.
