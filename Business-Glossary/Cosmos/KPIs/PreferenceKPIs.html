Customer Preference KPI

The Customer Preferences KPI gives deep insights into the customer’s inclination in their purchase pattern right from their choice of device, channel, payment-method to the products.

 

1. App Flag KPI: 

This KPI will typically be 1 for customers who use Mobile App (Android or iOS) for making purchases 65% of the time.

2. Desktop Flag KPI: 

This KPI will be 1 for customers who use Web or Desktop for making purchases 65% of the time.

3. Login Flag KPI: 

This KPI is 1 for customers who are typically logged in while browsing through the website.

4. App Purchase KPI: 

Customers who have made purchases through the App more than 50% of the time.

5. Store of Choice KPI: 

The store visited most times in the last 24-month period by the customer.

6. Channel Preference KPI: 

The customer’s preferred channel for the purchase. Does the customer visit store, order online and picks-up in store, or get it delivered?

7. Favorite Category Score KPI: 

A score given to each Category for a unique combination of Customer ID and Category ID taking into consideration a customer’s transaction and browsing (clickstream) history with 80% and 20% weightage respectively.

8. Payment Preference Score KPI: 

A score given to each Payment Method for a unique combination of Customer ID and Category ID taking into consideration a customer’s transaction and browsing (clickstream) history with 80% and 20% weightage respectively

9. Preferred Month KPI: 

Based on historical data, the specific Month of the Year when customer buys more.

10. Preferred Day KPI: 

Based on historical data, the specific Day of the Week when customer buys more.

11. Color Preference Score KPI: 

A score given to each Color for a unique combination of Customer ID and Category ID taking into consideration a customer’s transaction and browsing (clickstream) history with 80% and 20% weightage respectively

12. Fit Preference Score KPI: 

A score given to each Fit for a unique combination of Customer ID and Category ID taking into consideration a customer’s transaction and browsing (clickstream) history with 80% and 20% weightage respectively.

13. Size Preference Score KPI: 

A score given to each Size for a unique combination of Customer ID and Category ID taking into consideration a customer’s transaction and browsing (clickstream) history with 80% and 20% weightage respectively.

14. Style Preference Score KPI: 

A score given to each Style for a unique combination of Customer ID and Category ID taking into consideration a customer’s transaction and browsing (clickstream) history with 80% and 20% weightage respectively.

15. Self-Family Usage KPI: 

Customers who order rich variety of clothes for the family and not just for self- consumption.

16. Distance to Fav Store KPI: 

Distance from the store where the customer transacts most.

17. Omnichannel KPI: 

Customer is store only, omnichannel, or online only.
