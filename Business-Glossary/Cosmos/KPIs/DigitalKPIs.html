Digital KPI

Digital KPI gives insights into cyber-behavior of the customers including their browsing and buying pattern.

 

1. Casual Browser KPI: 

Customers who make multiple visits to the website without an agenda to purchase but to be updated with various style options.

2. Impulsive Buyer KPI:

Customers who begin and end the session quickly and purchase using faster check out options such as apple pay etc., 

3. Inspired by Visuals KPI: 

Customers who check and spend time on different pictures and are constantly inspired by visuals for their style choices. Image clicks/Total clicks are considered for this KPI.

4. Open to Recommendations KPI: 

Customers who click on suggested items on the website and purchase, and the customers clicking on frequently bought together.

5. Goal oriented KPI: 

Customers who come for a specific product and search till then find the item of choice and then checkout. They immediately exit if they cannot find what they came for.

6. Minimal App Usage KPI: 

Customer who has installed the App, but no order has been placed in the 6 months and no clickstream sessions activity.

7. Risk Averse KPI: 

Customers who never purchase any new items without reading reviews and feedback. 

8. Impatient Browser KPI:

Customer who spends very less time on the App/Web across pages and exits. The bounce rate in these cases is very high.

9. Online Heavy User KPI:

Customer who has an average session time above 1.5* (average session time of all app/web users)
