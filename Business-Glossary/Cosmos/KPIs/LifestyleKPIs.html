Customer Lifestyle KPI: 

Customer Lifestyle KPI gives insights into customer’s routine, habits and their way of life that would determine their purchase-pattern.

 

1. Thrifty Deal Seeking KPI: 

Customers who use Coupons to make Purchases more than 50% of the time.

2. Fashionista KPI: 

Customers who are open to try out newly launched products (threshold is 60 days) in more than 50% of the purchases. 

3. Conservative KPI: 

Customers who tick to basics, and aren’t open to changing color, style, or design. 

4. Easy Returns KPI: 

Customers who order multiple items and return either in stores or through carrier services.

5. Time Sensitive KPI: 

Customers who want faster shipment methods, typically visits, or purchases only they really need them.

6. Average LTR KPI: 

The average likelihood that the customer would recommend/Rate a product they have purchased.
