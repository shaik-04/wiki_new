Marketing KPI: 

The Marketing KPI gives insights into promotions and their effectiveness in making the customers visit the app/web and purchase more often.

 

1. Marketing Channel Preference KPI: 

Web traffic source for each visit from different marketing partner channels.

2. Cross Channel Count KPI:

 The count of Distinct Campaign channels the customer has entered to perform activity in Web/App.

3. Email List KPI:

Valid distinct Email-Ids for the same customer ID.

4. Days Since Email Trigger KPI: 

Identifies how long it has been since a customer is triggered by a marketing partner to do Web/app activity. 

5. Email Open Rate KPI: 

How many emails have been after being sent? This KPI will be a relative showcase either in Decile scores or Buckets like (High, medium, and low).

6. Email CTR Rating KPI: 

How many emails clicking after being opening? The Variable will be a relative showcase either in Decile scores or Buckets like (High, medium, and low).

7. Average Communication KPI: 

Frequency with which the customer had been contacted - Email and Text.

8. Coupon Redemption Rate KPI: 

Number of coupons utilized in purchases / Total Number of coupons received by the customer.

9. Engaged Conversion Rate: 

The number of purchases after Marketing Trigger / Total number of purchases by the customer.
