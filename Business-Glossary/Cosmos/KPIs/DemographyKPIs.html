Customer Demography KPI

The Customer Demography KPI gives insights into the customer’s basic details like Age, Gender, Income, Occupation, Marital Status, Ethnicity, Address.

 

1. Age KPI: 

The generation bucket the customer belongs to. This KPI is calculated by

(Current Date – Date of Birth)

2. Income KPI: 

Annual family income of the customer.

3. Gender KPI: 

Gender of the customer.

4. Occupation KPI: 

Student / Desk-workers / White-collar employees / Entrepreneurs.

5. Marital Status KPI: 

The marital status of the customer.

6. Urbanicity KPI: 

Describes where the customer is from – urban/semi-urban/rural.

7. Commute KPI: 

Does the customer commute to office using public transport?

8. Has Kids KPI: 

Number of kids the customer has

9. Ethnicity KPI: 

Describes the ethnicity of the customer – Latino, Black, Caucasian, Indian, Chinese.

10. House-hold Size KPI: 

Number of people at the house.

11. House-Location KPI: 

Zip Location of the where the customers are posted.
