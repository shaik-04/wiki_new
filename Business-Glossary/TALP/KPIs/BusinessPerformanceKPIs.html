Business Performance KPIs

Business Performance KPIs give deep insights into various important metrics like Sales, Transactions, Basket Size, Market Share etc., These KPIs help businesses understand past performances and take optimal decisions that improves business health on the long run. 

 

1. Sales KPI: 

The Total Sales of a store for pre-period. Sales KPI is calculated by

Sum(sales)

 

2. Visits KPI: 

The Total no of unique transactions. Visits KPI is calculated by

Count(Distinct(transaction ID))

 

3. Units KPI: 

The Total quantities sold. Units KPI is calculated by

Sum(units)

 

4. Average Retail Unit KPI: 

The ARL is the mean sales per unit. The ARU KPI is by 

(Total Sales / Total units sold)

 

5. Basket Size KPI: 

Basket Size KPI is the average sales per transaction. This KPI is calculated by

(Total Sales / Total number of Unique Transactions)

 

6. Market Share KPI:

Market Share KPI is derived from the ratio of store-specific sales to the overall sales. 

(Individual Store Total Sales / Total Sales) * 100
