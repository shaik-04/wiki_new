Store Operation KPIs

Store Operation KPIs give deep insights into various parameters which shows how the store has been functioning in the past and thus take crucial in-store decisions that increases productivity, profitability and improves business health on the long run. 

 

1. Out of Stock KPI: 

The number of SKUs that are out of stock out of all available SKUs. The formula used is 

(No. of SKUs that are out of stock / Total No. of SKUs) *100

2. Labour Hours KPI: 

The total Labour Working hours for a store.

 

3. Labour Cost KPI: 

The total cost spent on Labour for a store.

 

4. Cost to Serve KPI: 

The total cost-to-serve.

 

5. FTE KPI: 

FTE KPI gives the number of full-time employees working in a store.

 

6. Employees KPI: 

Total number of employees working in a store.

 

7. Average Tenure KPI: 

Average Tenure of an employee in a store is given by

SUM(Avg Tenure * Full Time Employees) / Total No. of FTE
