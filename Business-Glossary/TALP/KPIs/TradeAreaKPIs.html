Trade Area KPIs

Trade Area KPIs give deep insights into characteristics of the area around a store or network of stores.

 

1. Population Density KPI: 

Population Density of a store is given by

(Total Population Covered by the store / Area Covered by the store)

 

2. Average Income KPI: 

Average Income of Customers.

 

3. Urbanicity KPI: 

Urbanicity KPI shows if the store is located in Urban or Rural.

 

4. Household KPI: 

The number of households covered by a store.
