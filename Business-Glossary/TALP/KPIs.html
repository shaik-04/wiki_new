Business KPIs

The following topics provide a quick reference to how each KPI is used, where each fits into the Cosmos Solution what question it helps answer, the calculation used to figure it, and how it is populated.

 

Business Performance KPIs:

Business Performance KPIs give deep insights into various important metrics like Sales, Transactions, Basket Size, Market Share etc., These KPIs help businesses understand past performances and take optimal decisions that improves business health on the long run. 

 

Customer Experience KPIs:

Customer Experience KPIs give deep insights into cognitive, affective, sensory, and behavioral consumer responses during various stages of the consumption.

 

Store Operation KPIs:

Store Operation KPIs give deep insights into various parameters which shows how the store has been functioning in the past and thus take crucial in-store decisions that increases productivity, profitability and improves business health on the long run. 

 

Trade Area KPIs:

Trade Area KPIs give deep insights into characteristics of the area around a store or network of stores.
