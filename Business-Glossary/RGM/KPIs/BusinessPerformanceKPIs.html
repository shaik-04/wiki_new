Business Performance KPIs

Business Performance KPIs give gain deep insight into various important metrics like Revenue, Profit, Sales, Margin, Growth and Market Share. These KPIs help businesses understand past performances and take optimal decisions that improves business health on the long run. 

 

Revenue KPI

Dollar value of sales of products to retailer. Revenue KPI is calculated by

(Price at which items are sold to Retailers * Sales Volume)

 

2. Profit KPI:

Profit KPI gives the total Profit gained. This is calculated by

(Revenue - Cost Incurred)

 

3. Sales Volume KPI:

Sales Volume KPI gives the Quantity of product sold.

 

4. Margin Percentage KPI:

Margin Percentage KPI gives the percentage of Profit gained to the overall Revenue. This is calculated by

(Profit / Revenue) * 100

 

5. Retailer Sales KPI:

Retailer Sales KPI explains the Dollar value of sales of products from Retailer to end customer. This KPI is calculated by:

(Price at which Retailer sells to the End Customer / Sales Volume)

 

6. Retailer Profit KPI:

This is the profit gained by Retailer.

 

7. Retailer Margin KPI:

Margin Percentage KPI gives the percentage of Profit gained by the Retailer to the total Sales of the Retailer. This KPI is calculated by:

(Retailer Profit / Retailer Sales) *100

 

8. Market Share KPI:

Market Share KPI is the proportion of CPG Sales w.r.t Total Sales. The formula for calculation is:

(Total CPG Sales / Total Sales across all Categories) x 100

 

9. Category Growth Rate KPI:

This explains the YoY change of category sales volume (CPG + Competitor products within the same category). The formula for calculation is:

[(Category Sales Volume of Current Year - Category Sales Volume of Previous Year) / Category Sales Volume of Previous Year] * 100

 

10. CPG Growth Rate KPI:

This explains the YoY change of retailer sales volume (only CPG products).

[(Retailer Sales Volume of Current Year - Retailer Sales Volume of Previous Year) / Retailer Sales Volume of Previous Year] * 100
