Assortment KPIs

Assortment KPIs help businesses gather in-depth insights and analysis needed to refine their category assortment and maximize sales. 

 

1. WTD Distribution KPI:

Weighted distribution is proportion of category sales from stores where CPG sells w.r.t total category sales across geography. This KPI is calculated by

(CPG sales + competitor sales for stores where CPG revenue > 0) / (CPG sales + competitor sales for all stores)

 

2. Projected KPI:

Projected KPI is the Estimated metric value when recommendations are implemented.

 

3. Forecasted KPI:

Forecasted KPI is the Estimated metric value based on historical data.

 

4. Opportunity KPI:

Opportunity KPI shows the Improvement in revenue by implementation of recommendations.

 

5. Recommendation Type KPI:

This KPI shows the Metric optimized in the ML model to arrive at the recommendation.
