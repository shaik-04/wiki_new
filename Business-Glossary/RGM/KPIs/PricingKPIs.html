Pricing KPIs

Pricing KPIs helps businesses arrive at the optimal price point for the products in order to maximizes profitability by using market and consumer data. 

 

1. Value Share KPI:

Value Share KPI is given by the Proportion of YoY change in CPG sales w.r.t YoY change in total sales.

 

2. Price Elasticity Index KPI:

Price elasticity index of demand, defined at a product level is the relationship between the price and sales volume.

 

3. Price Change Impact KPI:

This is the Amount Spent by CPG for implementing pricing changes. This is more in the form of allowance to Retailer.

 

4. Base Price KPI:

Base Price is the Price at which the product would be sold if there are no discounts.

 

5. Baseline Volume KPI:

This KPI is gives the Sales volume obtained when the product is sold at base price.

 

4. Promo Price KPI:

Promo Price is the Offer/discounted price at which the product is sold during a promotion.

 

5. Non-Promo Price KPI:

This is the Price at which the product is sold when there is no promotion.
