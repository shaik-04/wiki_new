Promotion KPIs

Promotion KPIs helps businesses continuously improve the effectiveness of trade promotion strategies in order to handle Inventory levels and Maximize Sales.

 

1. Trade Spend KPI:

This KPI explains the Amount spent for implementing promotions which are more in the form of allowance to Retailer for price discount. This KPI might also include the amount spent on other elements like featuring ads etc.,

 

2. Lift KPI:

Lift KPI shows the Percentage Increase in Sales Volume as per recommended Promotions.

 

3. Return on Investment KPI:

ROI KPI is the Proportion of incremental profit w.r.t trade spend. 

 

4. Budget Utilization KPI:

This KPI is the budget utilized for promotion from the overall budget. This KPI is calculated by

(Budget used for Promotion / Overall Budget)

 

5. Revised Forecast vs Actuals KPI:

Revised Forecast is the Estimated metric value with changes to promotions as per recommendations. Actuals is the Estimated metric value based initial set of promotions.
