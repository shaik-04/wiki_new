Business KPIs

The following topics provide a quick reference to how each KPI is used, where each fits into the RGM Solution what question it helps answer, the calculation used to figure it, and how it is populated.

 

Business Performance KPIs:

Business Performance KPIs give gain deep insight into various important metrics like Revenue, Profit, Sales, Margin, Growth and Market Share. These KPIs help businesses understand past performances and take optimal decisions that improves business health on the long run. 

 

Assortment KPIs:

Assortment KPIs help businesses gather in-depth insights and analysis needed to refine their category assortment and maximize sales. 

 

Pricing KPIs:

Pricing KPIs helps businesses arrive at the optimal price point for the products in order to maximizes profitability by using market and consumer data. 

 

Promotion KPIs:

Promotion KPIs helps businesses continuously improve the effectiveness of trade promotion strategies in order to handle Inventory levels and Maximize Sales.
