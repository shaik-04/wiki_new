Business KPIs

The following topics provide a quick reference to how each KPI is used, where each fits into the SCCT Solution what question it helps answer, the calculation used to figure it, and how it is populated.

 

Inventory KPIs: 

Inventory KPIs help businesses balance capital investment objectives & constraints, and service-level goals over a large assortment of stock-keeping units (SKUs) while taking demand and supply volatility into account.

 

Transportation KPIs: 

Transportation KPIs help businesses analyze shipments, rates and the constraints to produce realistic load plans that reduce overall freight spend and gain efficiencies across entire transportation networks.
